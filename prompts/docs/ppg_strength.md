# PPG / Strength for Cyclists

- Session frequency: 1–2 sessions/week for cyclists in-season; 2–3 for off-season hypertrophy block.
- Exercises prioritized: Romanian deadlift, single-leg press, unilateral split squats, hip thrusts.
- Rep schemes for force: 3–6 reps at 80–90% 1RM with long rest.
- Hypertrophy block: 6–12 reps at 65–80% 1RM, 2–3x/week.
- Core: 10–20 min daily maintenance; dynamic planks, pallof press, anti-rotation.
- Mobility & prehab: glute activation, thoracic mobility, hip flexor stretching.
